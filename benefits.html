Why join?

Avail the most effective promotional, brand building, knowledge enhancement and business networking benefits for your organisation
Having an established and recognised Exhibition Industry Association to help the members grow their business.
Listing of your organisation on IEIA website with detailed company profile, exhibitions/ services/ facilities, social media & website links etc. for credibility and high visibility across the right audiences.
Members listing in various industry representations, brochures etc.
Best and fruitful Networking opportunities during IEIA events- Open Seminar, IEIA CONNECT, Women's Leadership Forum and others.
Enjoy preferential participation prices for attending events of IEIA's 22 affiliated international associations/organisations such as UFI (Global), IAEE (US), AFECA (Asia), SACEOS (Singapore), SISO (US), AFIDA (Latin America), AEFI (Italy), RUEF (Russia), SBE (Poland), SAACI & AAXO (S. Africa). KEOA (Korea), CENTREX (Central East Europe), IELA (Switzerland), AMPROFEC, MACEOS (Malaysia), AFE (Spain), UBRAFE (Brazil), PACEOS (Philippines), AKEI (Korea), TEA ( Thailand) and SLAPCEO (Sri Lanka), as announced at various times.
Receipt of industry updates and resources on regular basis.
First hand access to business opportunities shared through IEIA networks.
Promotional support through circulation of show reports across the industry database through mailers, social media and IEIA magazine- 'Exhibits'.
Receive latest industry related Tender notifications in your inbox, to help you tap potential business opportunities
Use of IEIA logo on your organisation's website, stationery and other collaterals.
Opportunity to participate in professional development courses including the globally renowned Certified in Exhibitions Management (CEM) Program and other educational programs/workshops conducted by IEIA.
Discounted tariffs for advertisement opportunities on IEIA website and publications.
Access to educative webinars organised for members.
Access to industry resources through website log- in.
Invitation to all General Meetings of the Association.
And many more......

Exclusive benefits for Ordinary members:

Besides the above listed membership benefits, Ordinary members avail additional exclusive benefits as listed below:
Voting and contesting rights for IEIA's Executive Committee positions.
Participation in few exclusive events like CXO Summit, or any such invite based event open to Ordinary members only.
Higher discounts or preferential participation in IEIA initiatives as floated from time to time.
