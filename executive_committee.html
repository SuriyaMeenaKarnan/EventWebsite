Mr. Sooraj Dhawan
President, IEIA, Founder Director, Falcon Exhibitions Pvt. Ltd.

A PGDM from Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta with specialization in Business Finance, Marketing and Strategic Management, Mr. Sooraj Dhawan is attributed with inception and sustainable success of several landmark Trade Shows in India, including International Engineering Sourcing Show (since 2012), InfoComm India (since 2012), IPHEX India (since 2013), Cap India (since 2016), Source India (since 2016), Logix India (since 2019) PlexConnect (since 2023).

He is the Director of Falcon Exhibitions Pvt. Ltd. A visionary business strategist, Mr. Dhawan specialises in International Trade development and feels that trade shows are the best way forward to business expansion. He is also a big advocate of ‘Make in India – Export from India and is an acknowledged specialist in organising International Buyer-Seller meets in India in areas, including Engineering, Power & Electricals, Automobile components, Rubber, Plastics & Chemicals, Pharmaceuticals, Textiles and Information Technology.

Mr. Dhawan is also a trained business mediator and empanelled with the Ministry of Company Affairs in New Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Kolkata for offering business mediation and conciliation

Ms. Sonia Prashar
Past President, IEIA, Secretary General, Federation of European Business in India, & Chairperson cum Managing Director, NurnbergMesse India Pvt. Ltd.

Ms. Sonia Prashar has been working in the Trade Fairs Industry for more than 21 years. At present, she holds the position of Secretary General at the Federation of European Business in India and is also the Chairperson cum Managing Director NurnbergMesse India Pvt. Ltd. (100% subsidiary of NurnbergMesse GmbH). NurnbergMesse is one of the 15 largest trade fairs companies in the world and among the top ten in Europe. The portfolio covers some 120 international exhibitions and congresses at the Nurnberg location and worldwide.

Ms. Prashar is also the Chairperson, Advisory Committee, Women’s Leadership Forum, formed by IEIA in association with IAEE.

A Science Graduate with specialization in Education & German Language, she is responsible for the Chamber’s active role in Indian Trade shows in all the regions and promoting ‘Trade Fairs’ as an effective tool for marketing, while looking out for new possibilities and making a judicious selection in the changing market. Since the inception of this activity, she has handled the German participation at more than 150 shows (Automotive, Electrical, Engineering, Chemicals & Pharmaceutical, Machine Tool, Analytical Instrumentation & Biotechnology, Aero, Defence & Aviation, Beauty, Home Care & Personal Care etc.).

NurnbergMesse India under the leadership of Ms. Prashar, is today one of the fastest-growing trade fair companies in India. Its current portfolio includes 16 shows spread across various industry verticals and occupying one of the leading positions.

Mr. Ravinder Sethi
Vice President, IEIA, Chairman cum Managing Director, R E Rogers India Pvt. Ltd.

Born in Cairo, Mr. Ravinder is the son of an Indian career diplomat. Having spent most of his early life around the world, he ended up doing his Bachelors in Economics (Hons) from Hindu Collage and Masters in Business Economics from Delhi School of Economics, University of Delhi.

He ventured into the exhibition logistics arena in 1977, and a few years later in 1986, went on to establish R.E. Rogers India Pvt. Ltd along with his wife, Kiran. Apart from the logistics service, Mr. Sethi and his company, R.E. Rogers, play a dynamic role in the exhibition industry and have reached new benchmarks, both in India and on international turf.

He has been the past Chairman of IELA (the International Exhibition of Logistics Association), founder - member and presently on the Executive Committee of the IEIA (Indian Exhibition Industry Association), Vice president, AFECA, Chairperson of Industry Partners Working Group, UFI and Chairperson of ‘The Committee’, IELA. He is also the Vice Chairman of IAEE India Chapter.

Mr. Jagdish Patankar
Honorary Secretary, IEIA Co-Founder & Executive Chairman, M M Activ Sci-Tech Communications Pvt. Ltd.

Jagdish Patankar is the Co-Founder & Executive Chairman of MM Activ Sci-Tech Communications, India's leading company in Technology Events, Branding & Corporate Communications, B2B Media and India Partnering Services.

After his PG from Symbiosis Institute of Business Management,Mr. Patankar started his career with India Today at Delhi & Indian Express and soon after floated his DM and Advertising firm Market Missionaries India Pvt. Ltd. which grew to become one of the top 40 Agencies of India ranked by A&M. His experience in media and brand building cuts across sectors like FMCG, Automobile, IT & Technology, TV Channels, Banks, Transport, Real Estate, Gems & Jewellery etc.

Inspired by leading scientists of India at the beginning of a new millennium, Mr. Patankar incepted first Research and Technology showcase of India during Indian Science Congress which has known to become flagship exhibition of the country. In the last two decades, he has curated over 100 national and international B2B events in the fields of IT & Electronics, Nanotechnology, Renewable Energy, Aerospace & Defence, Public Transport, Agrotech, Biotech, Food & Nutraceuticals, Higher Education, R&D and UN SDGs. These events have orchestrated interlinking industry, research, academia and policymakers. He has also been instrumental in attracting International Trade Shows, Conferences and Industry Delegations to India and vice-a-versa to build global partnerships.

Mr. Patankar is a publisher of leading print & digital media like BioSpectrum, NuFFooDS Spectrum and AgroSpectrum from India and Singapore. His group company also operates Mumbai’s only Integrated CIDCO Exhibition Convention Centre at Vashi.

Mr. Patankar is also a notable faculty, anchor & speaker on various fora. His other interest is in the field of entertainment and content creation for Cinema and OTT platform. He has also acted in a few well-known Marathi movies and TV serials.

Mr. P. Ramakrishna
Honorary Treasurer, IEIA; Chief Executive Officer, India Mobile Congress

With a proven track record of executing turnarounds in P&L positions, his career milestones include establishing the India Mobile Congress as a globally recognized event, startup work on Patented Green energy storage Technology, developing the 1st Digital Modem in India, and contributing to the deployment of the 1st cellular network in the state of Andhra Pradesh under JT Mobile (Now Airtel). Furthermore, he was part of the team that developed India's 1st lightweight Carbon fibre tower.

His cross-continental experiences encompass North and South America, India, and Asia, holding prestigious positions in organizations such as the India Mobile Congress, COAI, Aster Tele Solutions Inc, Aster Teleservices Inc(USA), and Digicon.

As the CEO of the India Mobile Congress since 2017, Ramakrishna has led the organization through six successful editions, transforming the brand from its nascent stages into the largest physical event platform in South Asia in the ICT, Emerging technology & telecom space. He has successfully steered the brand and strategy, fostering key relationships with tech & telecom companies and establishing a solid voice for the Indian telecom & technology sector on the global stage.

Additionally, he has served as the Principal Advisor at COAI, managing RoW Advocacy with Central, State, and UT authorities, EMF Advocacy, and implementing the National Broadband Mission and Broadband Readiness Index of States/UTs. His role involves overseeing relations with Government & Operators, Corporate affairs, Crisis management, Advocacy, Legal Inputs, Policy Inputs, and Implementation across multiple states and central agencies.

Prior to his current roles, Ramakrishna founded Mpraxis Technologies Pvt Ltd in 2006 and led as its CEO for over ten years. He was responsible for operations, sales, business planning, and bringing in operational efficiencies and green energy initiatives for telecom sites.

Ramakrishna holds a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degree from the University of Mysore, a testament to his technical expertise evident in his career journey. His work and influence have been instrumental in shaping the telecom and technology landscape in India and beyond.

Mr. Sudeep Sarcar
Executive Member, IEIA, Chief Executive Officer India Exposition Mart Ltd.

Sudeep Sarcar: Transformational Business Leader and Turnaround Specialist

Sudeep Sarcar, an accomplished business leader with over 25 years of industry experience, is the CEO of India Exposition Mart Ltd. An MBA from IIM Rohtak and a master’s graduate from IIT Kanpur, Sudeep is recognized for his expertise in turning around businesses and driving operational excellence. His leadership spans association management, venue ownership, and third-party event management, marked by a rare ability to translate strategic vision into tactical action.

Under his leadership since 2011, India Exposition Mart has achieved transformational growth, hosting mega-events like Auto Expo and expanding into India’s largest convention-cum-exhibition center. Sudeep has also championed initiatives such as an in-house exhibition academy, grooming the next generation of MICE professionals. His contributions have been widely recognized, earning prestigious awards including the National Tourism Award by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India.

As Hony. Secretary of ICPB and board member of IEIA, Sudeep’s influence extends across national and international platforms. His dynamic approach—combining strategic insight, innovative thinking, and strong stakeholder engagement—continues to shape the future of India’s exhibition and MICE industry.
